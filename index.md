**Etienne Démoulin** is a Paris-based computer music designer; he is both a musician, whose instrument is a digital or electronic tool, and a contemporary creative artist, whose goal is to bridge the gap between musical research practices (work by contemporary researchers) and creation. He produces mixed music works, focusing his research on alternative sound production techniques (embedded and distributed audio). He performs electronic music in concert, notably with ensembles such as Le Balcon and Les Percussions de Strasbourg. He provides sound for concerts and contributes to the development of new sound techniques for live performances. Étienne Démoulin is an active member of the Velcro sound design collective.
